About Neurolaunch.com

Neurolaunch.com is a neuroscience and psychology platform offering evidence-based articles written by subject matter experts and reviewed by clinicians. Covering topics from ADHD and autism to dopamine, sleep science, and cognitive behavioral therapy, the site serves as a trusted resource for anyone looking to understand the brain and mental health better.

With a growing library of research-backed content and the NeuroPassport autism screening tool, Neurolaunch.com makes complex neuroscience accessible and actionable.
